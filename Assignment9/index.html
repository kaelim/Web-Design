First Solo Trip

Instructable

What You Need:

  • Suitcase or backpack
  • Passport
  • Plane ticket
  • Clothes, toiletries, medications, ect.

Step 1: Decide where you want to go

Step 2: Get a Passport

This is only neccesary if you are traveling internationally. If you are traveling internationally and already have a passport confirm that your trip is not within the last 90 days of its vallidity.

Step 3: Book Your Flight

Now that you know where you want to go and have all the neccesary documentaion its time to book you flight. I recomend google flights to easily copare different airlines. This is also good to preview prices on dates if your on a budget.

Step 4: Book Accomidations

I recommend looking at hostels as tehy are affordble and are good for solo travel. You can get private rooms or dorms and the hostel setting will allow you to make friends and get ideas on things to do.

Step 5: The Fun Stuff

Now that you have all you travel details sorted it's time for the best part. Now you can plan any tours or activities along wit making a list of any sights you want to see. Leave room for reccomendations you get once you arrive from staff and possibly new friends.
