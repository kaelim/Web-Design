Pumpkin Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 can 14oz pumkin puree
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat overn to 325F.
  2. Grease baking pan with butter and flour.
  3. Mix the pumpkin, sugar, butter and vanilla.
  4. Add in the salt, cinnamon, baking soda and baking powder.
  5. Add the flour slowly and mix until combined and smooth.
  6. Bake for 65-75 minutes.
